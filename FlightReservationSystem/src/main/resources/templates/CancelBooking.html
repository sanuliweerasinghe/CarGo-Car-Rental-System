Online Refund Request

This FAQ section provides you information on the refund and cancellation of your tickets purchased via JetEase Airline.

  • The refund amounts will be calculated based on the conditions.
  • You can obtain a Travel Voucher valid for 6 months, so you have more flexibility to reschedule when you are ready to travel again.
  • The value of your travel voucher will be equivalent to the unutilized ticket amount you paid for your original booking.

**The cancellation/refund request must be made within 8 months of the ticket purchase to be eligible for the refund.
